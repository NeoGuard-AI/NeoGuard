About NeoGuard

NeoGuard is a cutting-edge tool designed to revolutionize maternal and fetal care. Our dashboard leverages advanced AI models and data analytics to predict the risk of Fetal Growth Restriction (FGR) at birth, empowering healthcare professionals with insights to provide personalized and proactive care throughout the pregnancy journey.

NeoGuard combines ultrasound biometry, Doppler blood flow, and maternal serum biomarkers to deliver a high level of accuracy in FGR prediction, ensuring early detection and intervention. With comprehensive data analysis, NeoGuard enables clinicians to make evidence-based decisions to enhance patient outcomes.

Trusted by medical professionals, NeoGuard is poised to improve the health and safety of both mothers and their babies.
